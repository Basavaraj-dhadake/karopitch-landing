About Karo Pitch

Built for Bharat's Builders

Thousands of founders across India are building incredible businesses — from rural D2C brands to deep-tech startups — but lack access to the right investors, mentors, and visibility. Karo Pitch exists to change that.

We connect driven founders with investors through curated, high-impact pitch events and an always-on discovery platform — giving every startup a fair shot, regardless of their city or background.

How It Works

From Application to Funding — in 4 Steps

A transparent, founder-friendly process designed to get you in front of the right investors.

1
📋
Apply with Your Pitch Deck
Submit your startup details and pitch deck through our simple online form. No complicated paperwork.
2
🔍
Get Shortlisted by KaroStartup
Our team reviews every application and selects the most promising startups based on innovation, traction, and potential.
3
🎤
Pitch Live to Investors
Present your business in a structured, closed-room-style pitch event in front of active investors and mentors.
4
📈
Raise Funding & Scale
Close your round, access mentorship, and get the visibility to grow your startup to the next level.
